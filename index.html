Who I Am

About Me

I'm a Graduate Research and Teaching Assistant at Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology (KNUST), Ghana, with a First Class Honours degree in Civil Engineering.

My work sits at the intersection of structural engineering, machine learning, and sustainable materials. I run active research on concrete characterization, structural health monitoring, and life cycle assessments — and I build data tools to make sense of it all.

Beyond the lab, I design and build websites for clients who need a solid web presence, and I'm actively pursuing a PhD in Civil or Structural Engineering at North American research institutions for Fall 2026.
